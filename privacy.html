Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2024

Your Privacy Matters

At ToolGoAI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and tools. The short version: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All our tools run entirely in your browser, ensuring your complete privacy.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Unlike many websites, ToolGoAI is designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to be crystal clear about what we DON'T do:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it through our contact form.
  • Usage Data: We do not track which tools you use, how often you use them, or what data you process with our tools.
  • File Content: Any files you upload or process using our tools remain entirely on your device. We never see, store, or have access to your files.
  • Location Data: We do not collect or track your geographic location.
  • Device Information: We do not collect information about your device, browser, or operating system.

2. How Our Tools Work

All tools on ToolGoAI are built using client-side technology, which means they run entirely in your web browser. When you use any of our tools:

  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers
  • Your files and text never leave your computer
  • Results are generated instantly without any network communication
  • You can even use our tools offline once the page is loaded

This architecture ensures that your data remains completely private and secure. We physically cannot access your data because it never reaches our servers.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

ToolGoAI uses minimal cookies and local storage for essential functionality only:

Essential Cookies

We may use essential cookies to remember your preferences, such as theme selection or tool settings. These cookies:

  • Are stored only on your device
  • Do not track your behavior
  • Can be deleted at any time through your browser settings
  • Do not contain any personal information

Third-Party Cookies

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense and Adsterra. These advertising partners may use cookies to serve relevant ads. These third-party cookies are governed by the respective privacy policies of:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Adsterra Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or using browser extensions that block advertising cookies.

4. Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, you voluntarily provide information such as your name, email address, and message. This information:

  • Is used solely to respond to your inquiry
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is not used for marketing purposes
  • Is deleted after your inquiry is resolved

5. Third-Party Services

ToolGoAI may contain links to external websites or use third-party services for specific functionality:

Advertising Networks

We partner with Google AdSense and Adsterra to display advertisements. These services may collect information about your visits to our website and other websites to provide relevant advertisements. We do not control these third-party cookies or tracking technologies.

External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

6. Children's Privacy

ToolGoAI is designed to be safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect personal data from any users, this policy applies universally. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information through our contact form, please contact us, and we will delete such information promptly.

7. Data Security

While we don't collect or store your data, we still take security seriously:

  • Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit
  • All tools run client-side, eliminating server-side vulnerabilities
  • We regularly update our codebase to address security issues
  • We follow industry best practices for web security

8. Your Rights and Choices

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there's no data for you to access, modify, or delete. However, you have the following rights:

  • Browser Settings: You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings
  • Ad Preferences: You can opt out of personalized advertising through ad network settings
  • Contact Requests: You can request deletion of any information submitted through our contact form

9. International Users

ToolGoAI is accessible worldwide. Since all processing happens in your browser and we don't collect data, there are no international data transfer concerns. Our privacy practices comply with major privacy regulations including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act).

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Significant changes will be prominently announced on our website
  • Your continued use of ToolGoAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

11. Google AdSense Compliance

As a website displaying Google AdSense advertisements, we comply with Google's policies:

  • Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings
  • You can also opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting aboutads.info

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@toolgoai.com

Contact Form: Visit our Contact Page

We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.
